(110-280) Changdeokgung 1 gil 18, Chongro-Gu, Seoul, Korea

Location

LG Sangnam Library is close to Changdeokgung and the Hyundai Corporation Building. You can get to the library by getting off at An-guk station on Orange Line of subway.
Use exit gate no. 3 of the station which is on the way to the Hyundai Corporation Building. It takes 5-10 minutes to walk from the exit to the Library.
