LG사이언스랜드

LG ScienceLand provides fun and exciting educational contents about science for children and teenagers.

Go to LG ScienceLand

A Fun and Exciting Learning Space to Enjoy Science

LG ScienceLand is a web portal-site for the youth of Korea. According to the slogan, “an interesting Cyber Science Land’, it produces and off- ers scientific multi-media content - interesting and fun Science Songs, Scientific Motion Pictures in daily lives, Science Games, and Laborato- ry Experiments Motion Pictures which everybody can easily follow. While following the step-by-step instructions, users can not only learn scientific knowledge but also clarify how to solve problems and boost thinking power and creativity.

A Information Service Integrating Scattered Science Information on the Web

LG ScienceLand offers an integrated search service that was designed to effectively search and easily gather scientific information that is usu- ally too scattered on the Web to collect and use. Its purpose is to provide outstanding content that encourages young people to take an interest in Science over and above what they learn in school.

LG ScienceLand offers 25 services in 6 categories - not only for children and students, but also for teachers and parents.

Science Songs

Users can learn science course material by singing along with the science songs that come with fun animation. They can also share their own user-created science songs with friends.

Science Experimentation

Now our users can experiment virtually in LG ScienceLand. It offers science experiment videos and flash contents that visu-alize the actual science experiment.

Science Games/Comics

LG ScienceLand offers science comics and games. By reading comics and playing games, users can learn science principles by heart.
