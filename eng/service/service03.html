책 읽어주는 도서관

The LG DTB Library, world’s first ubiquitous library, provides heartwarming information service tailored to the needs of print-disabled users.

Go to LG DTB Library

The world's first ubiquitous library for the print-disabled

The LG DTB (Digital Talking Book) Library was launched on the 10th anniversary of the LG Sangnam Library in order to expand the recourses of information available to the disadvantaged. It’s an audio library specifically designed for the print-disabled to easily access the library and listen to talking books at anytime from anywhere via the Internet, telephones or mobile phones. This new technology opened up a whole new possibilities for the disabled in the Information era.

A heartwarming service actively utilizing cutting-edge technologies

The LG DTB Library was developed by the LG Group as a social contribution under the motto “to realize heartwarming ubiquitous technologies”. Four companies retaining ubiquitous technologies in the LG Group (LG Electronics, LG U+, LG CNS, LG Innotek) have cooperated with the LG Sangnam Library in the development process. To achieve this realization, we have applied cutting-edge technologies to the environment that the disabled encounter, to remove conditions in which their physical limitations hamper their reading activities and information literacy. Keeping to that spirit, we will do our best to realize the services that the disabled have always dreamed of.

Enables listening to high-quality Digital Talking Book(DTB) anytime anywhere

The Digital Talking Books of the LG DTB Library are designed to follow DAISY, the world standard of Digital Talking Books. Designed for the pr-int disabled, talking books are made up of diverse multiple functions including search, bookmark, speed control and so on.

The LG DTB Library provides over 50 books every month, consis-ting of new publications, instruction materials for masseuse and physical therapists, study materials. All of its audiobooks and services are free to access and use through web and mobile ph-one for registered members.

Web Service

Users can access our website with their PC. They can choose books from new materials, best sellers, recommendations. With ‘My DTB’, the LG DTB library offers personalized services like book request and personal bookmarks.

모바일 서비스

With mobile phones especially designed for the LG DTB Library, users can access the same menu as DTB website. Users can access the mobile library and choose whatever book they wou-ld like, and will be able to download and listen to the book no matter when and where they are.
