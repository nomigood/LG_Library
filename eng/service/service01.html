LG ELIT

LG ELIT is Korea’s first digital library service. It is a web portal for researchers in the field of science and technology.

Go to LG ELIT

The First Digital Library Service in Korea

The first information service launched by the LG Sangnam Library was LG ELIT (Electronic Library Information Tour.) From it’s inception LG ELIT has proven to be a solid aid in the field of Science and Technology.

A Portal Service Specializing in Science and Technology

It provides multi-dimensional scientific information including educational information, research information, technology trends, and subsidiary information as a portal for Science and Technology information. ELIT actively collects course materials from outstanding universities of the world and provides technology trend reports for professors, undergraduate or graduate students and academic full-texts via the Internet and provide technology trends and subsidiary information for researchers for institutions in the public or private sectors. In terms of members, LG ELIT welcomes anyone who is interested in the field of Science and Technology and they can be serviced a English thesis/paper proofre- ading service and we are providing well-organized tutorial specialized on how to write Good thesis or papers in English. Our member use LG ELIT as a communication center as well as an information service center.

Providing more than Library Service through Service Differentiation

LG ELIT has transformed itself in accordance with the needs of its users. Paying great attention to the voices of its members, LG ELIT has been a pioneer in its unique and distinct territory, and has adopted advanced Web technologies in an effort to improve its tailored services, search functions, and user interfaces.

LG ELIT provides broad range of science and technology inform-ation by combining the holding collections LG Sangnam Library had produced or purchased and external resources of other info-rmation services.

Course Materials

LG ELIT collects course materials of world-renowned universit-ies. We offer full text materials, including lecture note, referenc-es, and assignments.

Thesis Writing Tutorial / English Thesis proofreading Service

LG ELIT offers English Thesis Writing Tutorial, full of basic Eng-lish thesis writing skills. And LG ELIT provides English thesis proofreading service to researchers having hard time finishing up their thesis in English. The theses are proofread by English native speakers with a doctorate in science.

Technology Trends

LG ELIT conveys latest news in the field of science including up-to-date trends, industry trends, science blog, science video.
