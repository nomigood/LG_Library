Overview

The LG Sangnam Library was founded by the LG Yonam Foundation to function as the first digital library of Korea. The Library was established for the purpose of making a great contribution to research andTechnological advance ment in the field of Science & Technology.
The LG Sangnam Library was opened in a building that was donated by Cha-Kyung Koo, the honorary chairman of the LG Group. As Korea's first digital library, the library opened its doors on April 17th, 1996. Cha-Kyung Koo, the founder of the LG Sangnam Library, said at the opening ceremony, "I expect that the LG Sangnam library will be a springboard of information, an endless supply of useful materials for Korea's science & Technology community and a role model in proposing the future structure for Korea's libraries." In accordance with that expectation, he LG Sangnam Library has pioneered innovation and led Korea's libraries in attaining milestones in the field of digit- al library services.

Slogan

Providing a future-oriented web service platform.
Offering high quality user-centered science contents.
Responding actively to the ever-changing mobile web environment.

Korea's first Digital Library

Until June, 2000, the LG Sangnam Library had digitalized 1.68 million articles regarding Science & Technology, and had provided these digitalized materials through the Internet free of charge. This resulted in 2.26 million users making use of the digitalized articles a total of 5.66 million times. Since its opening around 5,600 library-related professionals have visited the library from over a thousand various organizations. This substantiates the status of the LG Sangnam Library in South Korea as the leader in establishing benc- hmarks and creating a model of the digital library.

A repository of precious information in Science & Technology LG ELIT

The copyright act in Korea was revised to reflect new transmission rights regarding digital materials in July, 2000. This change jeopardized the existence of digital libraries in the future. The LG Sangnam Library needed to be flexible when it coped with these changes. This resulted in the Library successfully changing into a portal site that specializ- es in Science & Technology under the name LG ELIT.

An interesting cyber science and LG SCIENCELAND

The LG Sangnam Library launched LG ScienceLand in order to make a contribution to solving of a serious social problem, which was that teenagers started to avoid studying Science and Technology after the year 2000. LG ScienceLand, a edutainment science portal site, was designed to entice the youth of Korea to take an interest in Science and build scientific minds.

The world's first ubiquitous library LG DTB LIBRARY

The LG DTB Library was developed as the world’s first ubiquitous library specifically designed for the print-disabled. This was completed in conjunction with the 10th annivers- ary of the LG Sangnam Library. Via the library services, the print disabled can listen to audio copies of books at anytime and from anywhere.
